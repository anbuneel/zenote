## 2025-05-27 - Reverse Tabnabbing Protection\n**Vulnerability:** External links (`target="_blank"`) were missing `rel="noopener noreferrer"`.\n**Learning:** `DOMPurify` does not automatically add `rel="noopener noreferrer"` to `target="_blank"` links unless explicitly configured via a hook.\n**Prevention:** Use `DOMPurify.addHook('afterSanitizeAttributes', ...)` to enforce this attribute on all `target="_blank"` links.
